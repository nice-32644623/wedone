Our Story

Diya Dilusso

Born from a love for quiet, considered luxury, Diya Di-lusso is a boutique cake studio by Chef Diya Modi. We create couture cakes and refined petits where flavour leads and styling follows—clean lines, precise finishes, and ingredients chosen for their provenance.
Every piece is crafted to order in our Zirakpur atelier for intimate weddings, milestone birthdays, and thoughtful gifting across the Tricity. Think vanilla-bean tiers with raspberry brightness, 70% cocoa ganache with a satin sheen, Sicilian pistachio praline—balanced, not loud.
From a brief consultation to a tailored sketch and final flourishes, our process is intentionally unhurried. Limited slots keep quality absolute. Luxury now has a flavour.
